Delete your Breathe account & data

Last updated: June 13, 2026

This page explains how to delete your Breathe account and the data we store for you. Breathe is operated by Humberto Pedraza.

How to request account deletion

  1. Sign out of Breathe on your device (Account → Sign out).
  2. Email hpedraza2333@gmail.com from the same email address used for your Breathe account.
  3. Use the subject line Breathe account deletion request. We will confirm when deletion is complete (usually within 30 days).

Delete data without deleting your account

Guest mode (no account)

Session history and preferences stay on your device only. Remove them by uninstalling Breathe or clearing the app’s storage in your device settings (Settings → Apps → Breathe → Storage → Clear data). No request is required.

If you are signed in and want specific synced data removed but wish to keep your account, email hpedraza2333@gmail.com with the subject Breathe data deletion request and describe what you want removed.

What we delete

DataDeleted with account?
Email address and authentication identifiersYes
Synced session history, streaks, and preferencesYes
Subscription entitlement records in our databaseYes (billing history may remain with Google Play / Apple)
Crash/diagnostic logs tied to your accountYes, where technically feasible

What we may keep

  • Billing records held by Google Play, the Apple App Store, or RevenueCat for tax, fraud prevention, and legal compliance.
  • Minimal logs retained only as long as required by law, then deleted or anonymized.

Retention

We process deletion requests within 30 days. Backup copies may take up to an additional 30 days to purge from our service providers.
